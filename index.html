About Us

Our Firm

EngTech Consultant together with its associates is a progressive consulting engineering firm providing civil, structural, geotechnical, processing and project management services to private clients, corporate clients and governmental agencies.

The firm is able to provide extensive range of engineering as well as project management services to a wide spectrum of economic sector ranging from Commercial, Industrial, Institutional, Residential Buildings (High Rise and Low Rise) to Agro Based Processing Plant.

Our Philosophy

EngTech is committed to provide quality professional services targeted at assuring client’s confidence, timely job completion and within budget.

We treat our Clients as our long-term business partners and acknowledge their priorities and needs. We emphasize on team work with our clients and co-consultants and together we will work towards ensuring best value for the development.
